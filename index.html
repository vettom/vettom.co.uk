Herts Toppers Badminton Club

Inclusive Badminton Club at Hertfordshire Sports Village, Hatfield

We are a friendly, competitive Badminton Club based at Herts Sports Village. Members are a mix of able bodied and Para-Badminton players. We welcome new members of all ages and abilities. All our games are played on feather shuttlecocks.

Some of our members compete in Herts league and in 2016 topped division
